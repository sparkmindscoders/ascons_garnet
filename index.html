Navi Mumbai

Why Pushpak Nagar?

Pushpak Nagar, Navi Mumbai’s upcoming growth hub, offers seamless connectivity to highways, airports, and business districts. With quick access to the new international airport and metro, it’s perfect for urban living and investment, blending convenience with a future-ready lifestyle.

International airport

Upcoming

Pushpak Nagar provides quick access to the upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport, making it a prime location for connectivity and urban living.

Quick Access to Mumbai

30 mins away

Enjoy a fast, 30-minute commute to South Mumbai with the upcoming Mumbai Trans Harbour Link, enhancing Pushpak Nagar’s appeal.

Nearest Railway Station

10 mins away

The Khandeshwar railway station is only 10 minutes away, providing convenient rail access and making travel easy and efficient.

Healthcare Nearby

Hospital

Pushpak Nagar offers health convenience with a 5-acre multi-speciality hospital in Sector 1, ensuring medical care is close by.

New Transport Links

Network

The proposed Panvel-Uran suburban line will add accessibility, further integrating Pushpak Nagar with the surrounding urban network.

Close to Kalamboli Circle

5 km

Pushpak Nagar is located just 5 km from Kalamboli Circle, offering convenient access to important routes and transportation hubs.

Luxury Accommodation

5-star hotel

A proposed 5-star hotel will bring luxury and hospitality services, enhancing Pushpak Nagar's allure as a destination.

Recreation and Leisure

Central Park

A planned Central Park in Sector 2 offers expansive green space for recreation and community gatherings within Pushpak Nagar.

Smart & Modern City by CIDCO

Modern Smart City

Developed by CIDCO, Pushpak Nagar is designed as a smart and modern city, emphasizing urban convenience and infrastructure.

Educational university

Education Hub

Sector 7’s proposed educational university will make Pushpak Nagar a hub for higher learning and academic opportunities.

Multi-Purpose Stadium

Sports Facilities

The planned multi-purpose stadium in Sector 9 offers a venue for sports and large events, enriching community life.

Metro Station Phase III

Future Metro Connectivity

Pushpak Nagar will benefit from a proposed Metro Station in Phase III, enhancing public transport options and citywide access.
